About Me

Since I can remember, I have always been active. When I say active, I mean that I never settled for less; I participated actively in many activities and kept those that excited me. In school, when my teachers informed my parents that there was room for improvement, I became determined and, in the end, succeeded, graduating high school with honors (18.9).

I am heavily influenced professionally by my father, who is a high-voltage electrician. When I was young, I used to say that I wanted to work with robots because they excited me (I am a fan of Transformers). However, I changed my mind when, from a young age, he took me along on some of his jobs, and then I insisted that I wanted to become an electrician.

When the time came to fill out the application for university and I saw my options, I weighed them and realized that innovative technology excites me. So, the job of a computer engineering and informatics engineer, managing data, and creating advanced applications was the job for me!

A significant impact on my professional personality has been the family business, which is an outdoor skating rink by the sea. I must say that as I grow older, I enjoy taking on more and more responsibilities in the business. I started at a young age as a skating instructor, and now my main duties are finding and evaluating seasonal staff and managing the social media.
